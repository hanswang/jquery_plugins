okTabs

Simple Tabs

Demonstration

Tabs are dependent on very little markup. The minimum requirements are:

  1. The tab interface must be made up of links contained within an element. By default, it expects a list of links.
  2. The anchors of the links must match the id of the content element that they correspond to.
  3. The tabular content must be contained in a parent element. It doesn't matter the type

As with everything else, the styling of the tab is up to you. These are just demo styles
